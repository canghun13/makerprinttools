3D Print Selling Price Calculator

Turn a known production cost into a defensible target list price without fixing the calculation to one marketplace or currency.

Inputs

Use the full 3D Print Cost Calculator result when possible.
Packaging or other non-percentage order costs.
Calculated after fee and cost.

Result

—

How the calculation works

Price = (production cost + fixed order cost) ÷ (1 − fee rate − target margin).

How to use this result

Keep every input in the same currency. Tax, shipping charged separately, refunds, advertising, and design royalties may need their own allowance before you publish a price.

Example

With 12 production cost, 2 fixed cost, 12% fees, and 25% target margin, the tool solves for a price that leaves the requested margin after the fee.
