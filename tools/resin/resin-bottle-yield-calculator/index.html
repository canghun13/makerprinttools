How the calculation works

Adjusted resin per print = model and supports volume × (1 + allowance). Full prints per bottle = floor(bottle volume ÷ adjusted resin).

How to use this result

Bottle yield is an inventory estimate. Include supports, and use an allowance for resin left on parts, plates, filters, and containers; this tool does not assume a brand-specific density or bottle format.

Example

A 1 L bottle may cover fewer than 15 nominal 65 ml prints once handling loss is included.
